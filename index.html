Welcome To MGM

Mahatma Gandhi Mission Trust was established on December 20, 1982, in Nanded, Maharashtra, with a futuristic vision to provide qualitative education and health services in the underdeveloped Marathawada region. Since its inception, the trust has focused on providing healthcare services, school education, and higher education with dedication and commitment. Over time, MGM extended its services to Aurangabad, Navi Mumbai, and Parbhani in Maharashtra, and Noida in Uttar Pradesh. A chain of schools and colleges in the faculties of engineering, architecture, medical, nursing, management, computer science and IT, biotechnology, bioinformatics, fine arts, and journalism stand testimony to the endeavors of the trust. Mahatma Gandhi Mission has been instrumental in promoting classical dance and music for art seekers. The Trust has also paid heed to physical fitness by promoting sports and creating facilities for them. Services to society at the grass-roots level have been the basic vocation of the trust, along with education. The Trust has provided prompt and efficient healthcare services to the economically weaker section of society. The Trust's hospitals and medical colleges underscore its commitment to human resource development, social health, and welfare.

Vision & Mission

Vision

"To empower the professionals to be technologically skilled, innovative, self motivated and become responsible citizens with research oriented approach for solving social and industrial challenges for betterment of society."

Mission

To foster skilled human resources through quality education, leading to employability of the students.
To develop quality professionals by providing state of art training and hands-on experience.
To nurture students by enhancing ethical and moral values, fostering creativity and innovation enabling entrepreneurial qualities.
To develop socially responsible students committed to self-reliant India through various extra-curricular and co-curricular activities.
